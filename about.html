About Prof. Gaetano Santulli

Prof. Gaetano Santulli is a Full Professor of Medicine with a focus on Cardiology. He leads a dynamic laboratory dedicated to understanding cardiovascular and metabolic diseases, bridging basic research with clinical applications. His work spans molecular cardiology, vascular biology, and translational medicine, emphasizing innovation, rigor, and mentorship.

Research Mission

The lab aims to uncover the molecular mechanisms driving cardiovascular and metabolic disorders, with a particular focus on the role of calcium signaling, mitochondrial function, and microRNAs in cardiac health. Clinical Interests

Clinically, Prof. Santulli maintains active involvement in patient care, specializing in complex cardiovascular conditions. He collaborates closely with clinicians and translational scientists to ensure that laboratory findings inform therapeutic strategies and improve patient outcomes.

Mentorship

Prof. Santulli is committed to training the next generation of scientists and clinicians. His mentorship philosophy emphasizes creativity, independence, and excellence, fostering a lab environment where team members can thrive academically and professionally.
